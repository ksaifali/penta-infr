Customs Broking

Swift, accurate customs clearance backed by deep regulatory expertise and authority relationships.

Our licensed customs brokers manage import and export filings with speed and precision. With established relationships at all major Indian customs stations — BOM, DEL, BLR, MAA, HYD, CCU — we minimize dwell time and reduce the risk of compliance delays.

From HS code classification and duty calculation to DGFT licensing and advance rulings, our team handles all regulatory requirements for your cargo category — including pharmaceuticals, chemicals, DG goods, and specialized project equipment.
